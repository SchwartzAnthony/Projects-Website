iOS App: A1 German

The App was developed to assist those trying to take the first German Language Effeciency Exam.

It offered five different ways to study: Listening, Reading, Writing, Speaking and Vocabulary.

Getting all answers correct in one of the nine categories rewarded a golden crown for completion.

NOTE: The App is currently offline since it didn't make back the cost to keep it up.
